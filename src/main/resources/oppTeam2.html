CHESS TEAM PROFILE AND STATS

Name:"The Force "
Created:24-Sep-11
Description:
FORCE is defined as any influence that
causes an object or thing to undergo a
change in speed or a change in shape,
state and form. It has both magnitude
and direction. Therefore it is a constant
movement, chain reaction, but never
stagnant. There are numerous forces in
the universe ... they can be harsh, lethal,
gentle, or even brutal and unforgiving.

There are 'forces' of Good and Evil ...
but we despise the latter. The good
ones are now being harnessed into
a single entity which is 'The Force'.
This is the ultimate team ... the joint
forces team. So if you think you are
'a force' to be reckoned with, you are
welcome to join and unite to become
one formidable force on GK.

May "The Supreme Force" be with you!

★ HISTORY OF THE TEAM ★
The Force originated on Sep2011. The Force was "re-created" by Captain Mickod (Sep2016/Aug2018). Mickod was as well Captain Dark Affair for masterminding its merging (Jul/Sep2018). Then was Senior Co-Captain/Special Adviser (Aug2018/Apr2019). Mickod was named in May2019 Honorary Captain in recognition of his outstanding Leadership.
